UF: VERG – Castleroid: 02

March 1, 2021

Continuing the tile-based retro graphics exercise inspired by Castlevania and Metroidvania design.

The Castleroid exercise is exactly what it sounds like — a deliberate study in the tile-based graphic language of classic Castlevania and Metroidvania games.

The goal isn’t to clone anything. It’s to deeply understand how those games construct a sense of place with limited pixels. Every tile is a decision about value, silhouette, and repetition. Doing this work by hand — without shortcuts — teaches things that reading about pixel art never can.

VERG is the internal codename for the project framework. These exercises feed back into Mecromage and other game projects, building a vocabulary I can draw on.

Episode 02 expands on the tileset established in the first pass, adding environmental depth cues and experimenting with animated elements.
