Honey Badger Decal Wallpaper

October 12, 2018

An original character design built around the motto "Never Quit!", turned into a decal and desktop wallpaper.

The honey badger is a fitting mascot for a “Never Quit!” motto — the animal is famously relentless. This design started as a decal concept and expanded into a desktop wallpaper because the composition held up at larger sizes.

Original character, original design. No references beyond the animal itself and a strong sense of what the motto should feel like visually.

The wallpaper is available to download — click the title and right-click to save the full-size PNG.
