WIP — Mecromage Animation Demo

April 1, 2017

A look at the animation assets from Mecromage, an unreleased side-scrolling Metroidvania.

Mecromage has been in development for longer than I care to admit. It started as a concept in 2013 and has gone through more iterations than I can count — which is both a testament to stubbornness and a record of how much my craft has grown.

The animation demo collects a set of sprite-based assets built in Adobe Animate CC: walk cycles, idle states, and combat motions. These aren’t the final designs, but they represent a significant milestone in moving from concept to something that actually moves.

The game is a side-scrolling Metroidvania. The art direction leans into a dark, mechanical aesthetic — hence the name. The character design balances fantasy and sci-fi in a way that feels personal rather than derivative.

More to come. Mecromage doesn’t die.
