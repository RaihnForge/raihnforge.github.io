SMS Graphics — Mercer Creek Church

September 30, 2020

Social media notification graphics for Mercer Creek Church — and what I learned from working with practiced professionals.

This project was an opportunity to grow into a graphic style I wasn’t already comfortable with. Mercer Creek Church had a clear visual identity and a consistent content cycle — which meant I was learning to adapt to someone else’s established system, not just executing my own ideas.

That’s a different skill. When you work within an existing design language, you have to study it carefully: the color palette, the typefaces, the spacing, the emotional register. Then you have to produce work that feels native to it, not like something grafted on from outside.

I’m grateful for the chance to learn from practiced professionals who have consistent content cycles. The feedback loop of recurring work — where you see the effect of your decisions over time and have the chance to refine — is one of the best teachers available.

The graphics were used across the church’s social media notification channels.
