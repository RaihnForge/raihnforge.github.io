The Ash Keepers of Season 22 — B2W × W3C Avatars

January 20, 2023

A look at the avatar set I created for the W3Champions and Back2Warcraft esports community for Season 22.

Season 22 of the W3Champions ladder brought another opportunity to create custom avatars for the Back2Warcraft esports community. These pieces are a small but meaningful part of the competitive scene — players earn or receive them as recognition within the community.

Each avatar in the Ash Keepers set was designed to carry a distinct identity while fitting cohesively into the visual language of the W3C ecosystem. The challenge is always to make something that feels personal to the individual while also reading clearly at small display sizes.

Working with the B2W and W3C communities has been one of the most consistently rewarding parts of my freelance work — the passion these communities have for Warcraft 3 is infectious, and it pushes me to bring my best to every piece.
