Cheermotes for BaseTrade TV

May 24, 2017

Hybrid pixel-art style cheermotes designed for BaseTrade TV's Twitch channel.

BaseTrade TV is a StarCraft 2 broadcast organization with a strong community presence on Twitch. Cheermotes are animated emotes tied to the Bits cheer system — small, expressive animations that play when viewers donate.

The brief called for something that bridged the pixel art aesthetic with modern emote readability. The result was a hybrid style: the chunky geometry and limited palette of pixel art, but with enough polish to work at Twitch emote sizes.

Designing for the constraints of cheermotes (small canvas, animation loop, limited colors) is a useful discipline. It forces economy and forces you to think in motion from the start, not as an afterthought.
