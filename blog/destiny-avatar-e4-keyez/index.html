Destiny Avatar: E4 KEYEZ

November 17, 2017

Building a custom rendered avatar around my Destiny PlayStation Network character.

E4 KEYEZ is my Destiny character — the Guardian I’ve sunk hundreds of hours into. This piece was a personal project: building a proper avatar for the PlayStation Network profile using the character as a foundation but pushing beyond what the game renders.

The challenge was staying true to the in-game aesthetic while adding enough hand-crafted illustration to make it feel like art rather than a screenshot. The Destiny visual language is dense — lots of intricate armor detail, dramatic lighting, and a specific color palette per class.

The result is an avatar I actually use. There’s something satisfying about carrying a piece of your own work into a game you play.
