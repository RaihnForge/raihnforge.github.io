Approach

How I think about game graphics work.

Production-Aware

Design for the broadcast, not the still

Overlay designs are tuned against the live in-game source — readable at 1080p over chaotic visuals, safe against HUD overlap, and re-keyable per event by the production team.

Systems Thinking

Marks that scale across surfaces

Player and team identities are built as systems — primary mark, mascot variant, watermark size, print lockup. The same brand has to hold from icon to billboard.

Shipped Work

Two decades, public surfaces

Most of this work was published live on Twitch and YouTube, distributed across team and event channels, or shipped inside my own indie games. The portfolio favors pieces with a public lineage.
