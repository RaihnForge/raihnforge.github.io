A superhero character generator built on the classic FASERIP system — the stat model from the original Marvel Super Heroes tabletop RPG (Fighting, Agility, Strength, Endurance, Reason, Intuition, Psyche). Roll a full character in a handful of clicks.

## Why FASERIP

FASERIP is beautifully compact. Seven primary stats, a ranked scale from Feeble to Unearthly, and a random-roll generator that produces characters with real texture in under a minute. It's an underappreciated system with deep expressive range, and it deserves better tooling than paper printouts and dice-by-hand.

## What It Does

- Roll full characters (stats, powers, resources, popularity, talents, contacts).
- Regenerate individual attributes without rerolling the whole sheet.
- Format output for a tabletop session — clean, readable, shareable.
- Fast enough to generate a roster of opponents mid-session.

## Status

Shipped and stable. Primarily a tool for my own tabletop table, but available for anyone who wants rapid FASERIP character generation.
