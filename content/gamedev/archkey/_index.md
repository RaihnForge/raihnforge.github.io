Archkey Studio is the studio I built after closing the book on Unchosen Paths. It runs on a different premise: that modern AI-assisted tooling closes the velocity gap that sank indie studios of the previous era. It's the production home of the [Mecromage rebuild](/gamedev/mecromage/), and the research ground for everything the rebuild depends on.

## Why a New Studio

The [Unchosen Paths postmortem](/gamedev/mecromage/#chapter-one--unchosen-paths-20092018) identified the core failure mode: ambition outran velocity because every hour produced roughly one hour's output. That's no longer the reality of game development. Code generation, AI-assisted art pipelines, procedural tooling, and rapid prototyping frameworks collapse the hours-to-output ratio. A solo developer operating with AI leverage can move at something close to what a small team produced a decade ago — and can ship.

Archkey is the studio structure built around that reality. It is learning-first, research-forward, and explicitly designed to feed the Mecromage pipeline.

## Operating Model

- **AI-assisted production, human-directed craft.** AI handles drafts, variations, boilerplate, and iteration. Creative authorship — *what feels right for this world* — stays with the director. The studio is organized so AI speeds execution without diluting voice.
- **Every prototype is a study.** Small, time-boxed builds that answer specific questions (tile silhouette language, enemy AI patterns, animation compression, game feel tuning). Studies compound; nothing is throwaway.
- **Governed by the [Forge Framework](/gamedev/forge-framework/).** Project/process separation, Four C's gating, the FORGE cycle. The framework exists specifically to prevent the patterns that ended the last studio.
- **Pipeline-first.** Tools, workflows, and automation are first-class deliverables alongside game content. A tileset generator, an animation pipeline, a level-prototyping harness — these are studio outputs, not side quests.

## Current Tracks

- **Mecromage Rebuild** — foundational assets being ported to the modern pipeline. AI-assisted asset expansion, procedural level tooling, new engine substrate. Project state lives on the [Mecromage page](/gamedev/mecromage/).
- **U2DTS — Unreal→DirectX Tileset Study** (formerly VERG-Castleroid) — a deliberate study in retro tile-based graphics, using Castlevania as the reference point for how tile grids create place, dread, and wonder. Feeds directly into Mecromage's environment system.
- **NDLZ — The Sixth Prime** — the JS13K collaborative prototype from 2022. Preserved here as a case study in what a constrained, focused, short-runway game build looks like when the pipeline is tight.
- **Phaser 3 experiments** — rapid prototypes that test mechanics before they touch the main project.

Archkey doesn't exist to chase shipped titles in its own name. It exists to finish Mecromage, and to make the studio infrastructure that lets the next project after that not take another decade.
