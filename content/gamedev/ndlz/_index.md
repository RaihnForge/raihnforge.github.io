NDLZ – The Sixth Prime is a game built for JS13K Games 2022, a competition where the entire game — code, assets, and all — must fit within 13 kilobytes.

## The Constraint

13KB forces you to be deliberate about everything. No frameworks. No asset pipelines. Pure JavaScript, hand-optimized. The constraint is the point: it strips game development down to fundamentals and forces creative problem-solving at every level.

## The Collaboration

The project was built collaboratively with the explicit goal of using the jam as a vehicle for growth. We wanted to sharpen skills, deepen our understanding of game systems, and strengthen the working relationship — not just ship something.

## Play It

The game is available in the JS13K 2022 archive.
