I really enjoy doing artwork of all kinds.  Often I believe I spread myself too thin creating so many types of graphics across so vast a spectrum.  And then Twigly happens, and I remember that it's these kinds of joys that keep me making graphics.

It's not Just that Twigly is turned out to be a decent enough graphic, its that I was able pass a bit of joy to a friend.  This was my first true D&D experience, played out via the Roll20 web platform.

Twigly brought many laughs and many a conflicting encounter.  The player created a character that was memorable and highlighted why Table top gaming is still so relevant today.  Roll playing is much more than a game, it part strategy, socialization, and performance.
