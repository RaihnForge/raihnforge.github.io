B2WRip is a Twitch emote made for the [Back2Warcraft](https://back2warcraft.com) channel, the long-running shoutcasting home for Warcraft III esports. The mark is built to be readable at Twitch's tiny emote sizes (28px, 56px, 112px) while still carrying the channel's visual personality, so it lands in chat the moment a play goes south.

Designed as part of an ongoing graphics package for B2W, alongside a paired animated shoutout sequence that triggers on stream when the emote gets spammed during a moment of dramatic loss. The animation extends the emote into a short on-broadcast beat that closes the loop between chat and the show itself.

**Role:** Brand and broadcast graphics for Back2Warcraft
