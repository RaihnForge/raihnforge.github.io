I often times listen to talk radio while I work on my various graphic projects.  One of my favorite shows is the ‘Bill Handel’ show.  [Rich Marotta](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rich_Marotta) is a superiorly entertaining and talented voice on the show.  There is a lot to love about this man and his amazing career, but at times the listeners and others on the show don’t show very much respect for his well educated opinion.

This cartoon pretty much an inside joke to those who listened to the show during the election era of 2008.  Check out [Rich on the Bill Handel Show on KFI](http://www.kfiam640.com/pages/RichMarotta.html).
