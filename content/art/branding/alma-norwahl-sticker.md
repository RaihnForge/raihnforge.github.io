A sticker designed for the team at [Alma](https://getalma.com), the cloud-based Student Information System built by educators for educators.

The piece riffs on a small detail you notice the moment you walk into Alma's studio: the waiting room is full of narwhals. The narwhal is the right mascot for the company. It looks mythical, a fat happy dolphin with a unicorn horn, the kind of creature you'd swear had to be made up. But it's a real animal that genuinely exists in the world. That's the same shape as Alma's positioning: a school-technology company that genuinely cares about teachers and students sounds like a thing somebody made up to sell a slide. Alma is the one that turns out to actually be real.

"Norwahl" because the swag deserves a pun, and because the narwhal already does most of the work of saying what this company is.
