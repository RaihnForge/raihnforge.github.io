I'm Joshua Keyes — a freelance artist working in digital illustration, animation, logo design, game design, and web design. I grew up in Washington State and served in the United States Marine Corps, completing two deployments to Iraq as an infantryman.

After the Corps, I studied animation and transfer studies at Mount San Antonio College in California, then built a career at the intersection of visual design and the gaming community.

I've worked with design firms and startup companies including SWIRE and DIP LLC — taking on roles ranging from freelance artist and product producer to web designer and quality consultant. I've also volunteered hundreds of hours in graphics and web design for esports organizations.

## Associations

**Unchosen Paths** (2009–2018)
Indie game studio co-founded with a guitarist known for Castlevania remixes. I served as producer, artist, game director, and software designer — responsible for conceptual artwork and overall project vision.

**Team Liquid** (2012–2016)
Designed overlay graphics for professional StarCraft and DOTA 2 teams. My visuals were seen by thousands of daily Twitch viewers representing both the organization and its sponsors.

**Team Grubby** (2010–2013)
Lead designer for professional gamer Manuel Schenkhuizen's esports brand. Created the logo, website design, stationery, and merchandise graphics during the early rise of competitive gaming.

**WCReplays.com** (2005–2010)
Feature artist, graphics staff, and writing coordinator for one of the premier Warcraft 3 replay communities.

**DIP Enterprises Inc.** (2006–2009)
Started as a graphics artist, promoted to lead art designer. Worked on board game development, board design, packaging, and website assets.
