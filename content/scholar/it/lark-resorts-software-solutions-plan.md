Lark Resorts is a fictional client built for the IT 260 final project, a Colorado-based hospitality company maintaining two properties in Vail and Telluride and preparing to expand into Alaska and California. The assignment was to write an executive summary that the company's leadership could plausibly hand to a board, recommending a standardized software stack to support that expansion. What follows is that document.

## The Mission

We must reconstruct our technology processes and applications in order to facilitate a cohesive, robust, responsible, and efficient infrastructure allowing for future growth and sustainment in our next organizational evolution.

Three considerations frame the work. Facilitate agile change in software processes. Ensure support for expansion. Prioritize the security of clients and stakeholders.

Lark Resorts is committed to year-round access to nature through a diverse offering of outdoor activities, alongside restaurants, pools, retail, fitness, business space, and community centers. Luxury condominiums in four configurations, furnished and unfurnished, are placed with attention to views, community interaction, and respect for the environment. The organization's identity is built around that pairing of nature and hospitality, and the technology decisions in this document should serve, not strain, that identity.

## Need for Restructure

The current state of technology and process within the organization shows several issues. Most associates use Microsoft Office, but a small portion are on alternatives like Google Apps. Mixing platforms produces capability and security gaps. Licensing and storage are not standardized, which creates friction across administrative support departments.

Communication is the most visible concern. Lark Resorts' core values stem from supporting positive individual and team contributions, but the organization currently has no uniform communication platform from which to organize and resolve issues for clients. Database management, marketing, and client communications are similarly inefficient and decentralized, costing time and budget.

## Goals and Objectives by Stakeholder

Stakeholders require clear goals that offer strong solutions to their technology needs and assist in the expansion mission.

**The Board of Directors** requires flexible and comprehensive solutions that allow for agile implementation and connection with systems while expanding the organization. Accurate data management systems must provide market projections. Quality standards must remain consistent across the organization.

**Sales Force** requires software that allows global, always-online communication. High production value presentations are expected. Tracking clients through databases is necessary. The team needs to create marketing materials for print, online, and social media to maintain client engagement.

**Administrative Support** requires collaboration on control and development documentation from all locations as Lark Resorts expands. Tracking trades, registrations, and cancellations is key. Secure private cloud storage and version control will be a must. Current statistics and analyses are also a necessity.

**Property Managers** require booking and registration database systems that are consistent and dependable. Communication and coordination of associate projects and tasks are essential. Managing and expanding the client database is imperative. Inventory management for onsite resources is required for accountability and auditing.

## Analysis for Action

Across those stakeholder needs, four organizational elements require application standardization and support.

**Data management** addresses analytics, security, organization, and storage. **Communication** includes real-time conversation, traditional mail and email, notifications and scheduling, and document collaboration. **Marketing** encompasses persuasive presentation, print and web production, and tracking and understanding clients. **Management** requires control and development solutions for projects, scheduling, and training.

The path forward requires adopting a reliable and consistent software ecosystem that addresses each of these areas. With that foundation, stakeholders and associates can pursue their unique goals while the technical challenges that have emerged stop accumulating.

## Needs Addressed: Microsoft 365 as the Recommended Stack

After research and evaluation, Microsoft 365 Office Suite and its associated products are the recommendation. The ecosystem is dependable, innovative, and aligned with industry standards, and it covers the four areas identified in the analysis.

### Data Solutions

Microsoft Access provides database management for analytics, generation, and information gathering. Access is designed to work in tandem with Microsoft Excel, which provides statistical analysis and presentation-grade data communication. Microsoft OneDrive handles cloud storage at any scale, with version control built in, enabling collaboration across stakeholders, teams, and associates.

### Communications

Microsoft Outlook manages email along with individual scheduling. Outlook pairs with Microsoft Teams to set up and attend meetings. Teams handles voice and video conferencing, meetings, and real-time collaboration on a diverse set of document types. Microsoft Word is the tool for informational guides, processes, standards, and planning, and can be paired with Teams for concurrent collaboration and asynchronous review.

Microsoft 365 Office Suite and associated products are dependable, innovative, and surpass industry standards.

### Marketing

Marketing needs are best served by Microsoft PowerPoint, Sway, and Publisher. PowerPoint creates high-quality presentations and interactive learning, training, and guide production, and works inside Teams' real-time development. Sway allows quick development of web advertisements and informational pieces. Publisher supports the design of attractive print materials.

### Management

With the upcoming expansion, leaders, sponsors, and management teams require detailed tracking and project management tools. Microsoft Project is an industry-leading application for tracking work packages, resources, budgets, and schedules. Administrative associates and property managers will coordinate employee tasks and schedules using Teams. While Access can help manage employees and clients, Microsoft Bookings is a better fit for client scheduling, reservations, and appointments.

## Conclusion

The Microsoft suite is available, cross-compatible, and largely accessible through a browser-based platform. Those features ensure compatibility while mitigating expensive software and hardware update costs.

Microsoft's offering is the solution enabling Lark Resorts to resolve current challenges. Overcoming IT obstacles will enhance the organization's efficiency and effectiveness in accomplishing its mission of expansion and improved customer experience, and it remains in keeping with the organization's commitment to quality. The sky is the limit as Lark Resorts flies into the future on the Microsoft software ecosystem.
