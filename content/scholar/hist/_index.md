HIST coursework gives me the long view. The patterns of organization, conflict, exchange, and meaning-making that show up across centuries are the same patterns that show up inside a studio, a product team, or a creative practice. The history work feeds the worldbuilding side of my creative output and tempers the contemporary-tech-first instincts of the rest of the curriculum.
