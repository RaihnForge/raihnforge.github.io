ENG coursework at CWU is the deliberate practice of writing. Building arguments that hold up to citation, separating what I think from what I can defend, and finishing a piece that a stranger could pick up cold and follow. Most of what shows up elsewhere on this site — journal entries, project case studies, the Forge Framework manuscript — got better because of the work here.
