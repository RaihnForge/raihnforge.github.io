A refresh of the website for **All Out Entertainment**, a full-service wedding and event entertainment company in the Pacific Northwest — DJ/MC services, day-of wedding coordination, and photo booth experiences for weddings, reunions, and celebrations.

The current site is WordPress, with duplicated sections and a structure that doesn't lead a visiting couple cleanly from "who they are" to "book us." The refresh focuses on a tighter information architecture, a stronger booking path, and a presentation that matches the energy of the brand.

The card links to the current live site while the redesign is in progress — work is tracked in `_queue/alloutentertainment-redesign/`.
