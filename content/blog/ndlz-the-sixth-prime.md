NDLZ – The Sixth Prime was our entry for JS13K Games 2022 — a jam where the entire game, including assets, must fit within 13 kilobytes of JavaScript.

The constraint is brutal and clarifying at the same time. Every byte matters. You can't rely on frameworks or asset pipelines. You have to think in fundamentals.

We entered with a clear goal: not just to submit something, but to use the jam as a vehicle to grow. The collaboration was as much about strengthening our working relationship and shared understanding as it was about the final product. Building something small and complete together does more for a team than months of loose planning.

The game is available to play as part of the JS13K 2022 archive.
