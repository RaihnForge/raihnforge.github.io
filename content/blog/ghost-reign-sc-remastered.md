When Blizzard opened submissions for the StarCraft Remastered portrait contest, I knew I had to enter. StarCraft has been a constant presence in my creative life — through the esports communities I've worked with, the fan art I've made, and the friendships the game has facilitated.

Ghost Reign features the Ghost unit rendered in a painterly style intended to complement the remastered visual direction. The Ghost is a compelling subject — silent, precise, operating in the space between human and weapon.

The piece is a submission, not a winner, but I'm proud of it. It represents where my digital painting was at that point in time.
