Avatar commissions for W3Champions players are always personal — the avatar represents someone in a competitive community they care about. The brief for this one was clear: approachable, friendly, with strong visual presence even at small sizes.

Avatar design lives at the intersection of portraiture and icon design. It needs to work at 64×64 pixels but also hold up when displayed larger on stream or profile pages. Every element has to earn its place.

The finished avatar captures the player's requested tone and has been in use on their WC3 profile since delivery.
