About

Product Owner & Creative Producer

I'm a product-minded creator with two decades of experience shipping design, games, and digital products. I've led cross-functional teams, defined product vision, managed development roadmaps, and delivered shipped work — from esports graphics seen by thousands to a nine-year indie game studio. I bring the same rigor to product ownership that the Marine Corps taught me about leadership: clarity of mission, bias toward action, and care for the people doing the work.

Core Competencies

Product Strategy

Defining product vision, scoping features, building roadmaps, and making trade-off decisions that balance user needs with technical constraints and business goals.

Cross-Functional Leadership

Leading teams across design, development, and stakeholder groups. Nine years co-running an indie studio taught me that shipping requires alignment, not authority.

User-Centered Design

Designing for real people — from esports communities to church congregations. Every project starts with understanding who it's for and what they actually need.

Agile Delivery

Iterative development, sprint planning, backlog management, and continuous delivery. Practiced through years of game development cycles and client-facing creative work.

Visual Design

Professional illustration, branding, UI design, and art direction. Design isn't decoration — it's communication, and I've been fluent in it for twenty years.

Technical Fluency

Comfortable with HTML/CSS, JavaScript, game engines, and development toolchains. I speak developer fluently enough to bridge the gap between design intent and implementation.

I’m Joshua Keyes — a freelance artist working in digital illustration, animation, logo design, game design, and web design. I grew up in Washington State and served in the United States Marine Corps, completing two deployments to Iraq as an infantryman.

After the Corps, I studied animation and transfer studies at Mount San Antonio College in California, then built a career at the intersection of visual design and the gaming community.

I’ve worked with design firms and startup companies including SWIRE and DIP LLC — taking on roles ranging from freelance artist and product producer to web designer and quality consultant. I’ve also volunteered hundreds of hours in graphics and web design for esports organizations.

Associations

Unchosen Paths (2009–2018) Indie game studio co-founded with a guitarist known for Castlevania remixes. I served as producer, artist, game director, and software designer — responsible for conceptual artwork and overall project vision.

Team Liquid (2012–2016) Designed overlay graphics for professional StarCraft and DOTA 2 teams. My visuals were seen by thousands of daily Twitch viewers representing both the organization and its sponsors.

Team Grubby (2010–2013) Lead designer for professional gamer Manuel Schenkhuizen’s esports brand. Created the logo, website design, stationery, and merchandise graphics during the early rise of competitive gaming.

WCReplays.com (2005–2010) Feature artist, graphics staff, and writing coordinator for one of the premier Warcraft 3 replay communities.

DIP Enterprises Inc. (2006–2009) Started as a graphics artist, promoted to lead art designer. Worked on board game development, board design, packaging, and website assets.
