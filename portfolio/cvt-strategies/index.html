Brand identity and website design mockup for CVT Strategies, the consulting venture of a senior growth executive with 15+ years leading revenue and operations across education technology’s leading platforms.

The identity starts with the name: CVT — Credible. Valued. Trusted. A custom geometric monogram (delivered as vector SVG), a LinkedIn banner, business card concepts, and an ocean palette drawn from the founder’s San Clemente coastline carry the brand across every surface.

The site itself is a single-page composition built to the studio convention — no build step, one hand-authored stylesheet, self-contained assets. It frames the practice around three levers of growth (business foundations, team alignment, GTM execution), a distinct advisory-or-in-the-seat engagement model, and a career timeline sourced from the founder’s actual track record.
