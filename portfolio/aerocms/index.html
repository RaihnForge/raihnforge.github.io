AeroCMS — Website Redesign

AeroCMS · Managed Services & Hardware-as-a-Service Web Design & Development 2026 Delivered

A full redesign of the AeroCMS marketing site — a multi-page static rebuild of a dated WordPress/Divi site for a Pacific Northwest MSP. Clearer service architecture, a real team and partner story, structured plans and comparison tables, and a resource library — built mobile-first with a single hand-authored stylesheet.

A complete redesign of the marketing site for AeroCMS (Aero Computer Management Solutions), a managed-service and hardware-as-a-service provider based in Nampa, ID with offices across five states.

The current site is WordPress + Divi: a generic hero, four service pillars buried in long copy, and a story that the company’s own strategy materials told far better than the page did. The redesign rebuilds the full site as hand-authored static HTML/CSS — homepage, about (story, values, full team, partners, offices), four service detail pages, a Good/Better/Best/Enterprise plans page with comparison table, a resource library, a news index, and a complete contact flow.

Built to the studio convention — no build step, no bundler, one stylesheet, responsive throughout. Delivered to the client for review.
