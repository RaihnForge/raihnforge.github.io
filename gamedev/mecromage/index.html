A side-scrolling Metroidvania with a dark mechanical-fantasy aesthetic. In development since 2013 under Unchosen Paths.

Mecromage is a side-scrolling Metroidvania that has been in development, on and off, since 2013. It’s the longest-running creative project in my portfolio — and the one I’m most committed to finishing.

Concept

The name blends “Mechromancer” and “Mage” — the game sits at the intersection of mechanical and arcane. The protagonist navigates an interconnected world that rewards exploration and punishes complacency.

The art direction balances dark fantasy with a mechanical edge: heavy armor, industrial environments, creatures that blur the line between built and born.

History

Development began under the Unchosen Paths studio umbrella. Early work focused on concept art, environment design, and foundational assets. The game has gone through multiple engine iterations and design overhauls as my skills and tools have evolved.

Current Status

Active development. Sprite animation, environment tilesets, and core movement systems have all been prototyped. The design doc is living and evolving.

Mecromage doesn’t die.
