Mecromage

Role
Producer, Artist & Game Director
Timeline
2009–Present
Engine
Phaser 4 / AI-Assisted Pipeline
Status
In Progress

A 2D metalvania set in a world of dark mechanical fantasy. The longest creative thread in my portfolio: begun in 2009 at the indie studio I co-founded, and being rebuilt now through Archkey Studio.

Mecromage has its own home — a frost-and-iron tour of the realm, the mechanics, the heroine Kochay, and the long arc from 2009 to now.

→ Enter the Mecromage site

The full product page lives there: the concept, Kochay the Frostwolf cleric, the freeze · grapple · shatter loop, the world and its four pillars, and the three-chapter arc.

Below is the development log — the working record across the project’s three chapters: Unchosen Paths daily diaries from 2011, concept and asset work from 2013 through 2017, the dormant years, and the Archkey-era rebuild. Earlier entries are preserved as the historical record, and the newest work sits at the top.
