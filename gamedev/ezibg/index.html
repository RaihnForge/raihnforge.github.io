A curated collection of eye-friendly desktop wallpapers designed to reduce digital eye strain, with warm tones and gentle contrast.

EZi BackGrounds is a collection of desktop wallpapers designed around one principle: be gentle on the eyes.

The Problem

Most wallpapers optimize for visual impact — high contrast, saturated colors, dramatic compositions. That’s fine for first impressions, but if you’re staring at your desktop for eight-plus hours a day, it becomes a source of fatigue. Blue-heavy palettes disrupt sleep. High contrast causes visual strain. For people with light sensitivity or migraines, it’s actively harmful.

The Solution

EZi BackGrounds uses warm tones to reduce blue light, soft gradients to minimize contrast, and low-key compositions to reduce visual workload. The goal is something you can live with all day, not just admire for thirty seconds.

Available Designs

The collection includes 12 themed backgrounds:

  • Raihn4ever, WoodzTex, Tah2Win, RalphiZly, PahzX
  • KeyForge, iTale, FeelzGud, AlterHillz
  • CityChase, WarmForest, NeverQuit

Sources include original artwork, Unsplash photography, and visual references from games and entertainment properties.
