In Development

A PWA drink-order builder. Compose your exact coffee order visually, share a link, and let the barista (or your friend) get it right the first time.

My Drink is a small, focused product with one job: remove ambiguity from ordering a drink. You build your exact coffee — size, milk, sweetener, temperature, extras — as a visual composition, and share the resulting order as a link or image.

The Problem

Ordering a specific coffee drink, especially a complicated one, is a communication problem. You describe it; the other person interprets it. Somewhere between “oat milk vanilla latte, extra shot, less foam, 140°” and the cup in front of you, things get lost. Chat messages back to the house about orders are fuzzy. Gifts of coffee often miss.

The Approach

Visual composition over text. Sliders and icons instead of free-text fields. Share a rendered “drink card” that’s unambiguous at a glance. Work offline (PWA), install to a phone home screen, and load fast enough to build an order before the line moves.

Status

Early builds are running. The order-composition UI is the primary focus; sharing and barista-facing views come next.
