About this paper

An early career-planning exercise that lists prospective internship and employment targets across game studios, security tooling, higher education, and public-sector veteran services. Each entry pairs a career-search posture (website and LinkedIn) with a short personal note: existing contacts, prior interaction, intrinsic interest, and where the organization sits relative to the student's experience.

Why it matters to the practice

Career research only works if it is honest about the candidate's existing relationships and the organizations' actual market positions. Each entry here surfaces an honest reason the target is on the list, which is the prerequisite for any later cover letter or networking conversation. The exercise also reads as a snapshot of the early 2023 career landscape across games, EdTech, and security tooling, with notes that prefigure how the candidate would actually pursue each path.

Connections

Pairs with the RMT 379 Three Facts, Three Questions exercise, which goes a level deeper on a single prospective sponsor. Together they bracket the breadth-first scan and the depth-first interview prep. Identifying details about the current employer have been generalized; public-target organizations remain named since the exercise itself is a public career-research scan.
