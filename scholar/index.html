Scholar

The Scholar section collects coursework and longer-form writing, sorted by department. Each department has its own page with the essays and notes from courses taken in that subject. Published works are listed separately at the bottom.

The work here is intentionally academic in posture: drafts get refined, references get cited, and ideas are connected back to the studio practice when the connection is honest.
