Back2Warcraft Twitch Emote — B2WBoat

Medium
Digital
Year
2025

Twitch emote for the Back2Warcraft channel, used to flag a player heading to a watery or off-map demise during Warcraft III broadcasts.

B2WBoat is a Twitch emote built for the Back2Warcraft channel. Like the rest of the B2W emote family, it has to do its job at 28, 56, and 112 pixel sizes, so the silhouette and color blocking carry most of the work. The artwork sits in the channel’s running visual language alongside the other shoutcaster-specific emotes used during Warcraft III broadcasts.

Part of an ongoing graphics package for Back2Warcraft that mixes overlay coverlay assets, emote families, and animated shoutouts so the channel reads as one cohesive show across chat, overlay, and stream.
