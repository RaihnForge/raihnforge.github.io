Overlay for X-Com Stream

Medium
Digital
Year
2012

Stream overlay system and speed-painted operative avatar for an X-Com co-cast with Kayfix on Street Level Gaming.

This piece is from my earlier portfolio. The original hosted images may no longer be available.

A couple of weeks ago a friend of mine, Kayfix, and I decided to play a run through of X-Com with “Field Expedient Multiplayer.” The idea is that we run with two teams of four, and that all deaths are permanent (as stated in our code of conduct) and that we cast the xenomorphic extravaganza.

We hope to convey to those not in the know what X-Com means to those that are. Utilizing the overlays and a fair dose of over-excitement, we feel like we are approaching a stream that is to at least our satisfaction, if not to that of the general gaming public.

You can check out our episode playlist at Youtube.com/StreetLevelGaming.

As part of the process of developing the ever-evolving overlay elements, I made a speed painting of the X-Com operative avatar that became the default for all our units.
